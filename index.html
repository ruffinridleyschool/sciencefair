Calling all scientists!

The theme of this year's fair is "The Earth", and the Virtual Fair--for 6th, 7th, and 8th graders--is this Saturday, March 13th at 7PM. You will need to choose an Avatar to move around the fair and interact virtually, and we encourage you to try it out early to you'll be ready for the fair. Just click the "Virtual Fair" button above.

There will also be an outdoor, in-person fair on May 8th, 2021 at 9AM for the entire school, pre-K through 8th grade, even those who may have particpated in the virtual fair. Fill out the registration form to sign up.

Due to uncertainties surrounding the pandemic, the date, format, and location (virtual/indoors/outdoors) of the main fair in May has not been confirmed. The PTO will announce the fair date and location in the coming weeks or months. At a minimum, that fair will also be held virtually.

Please note: your project does not have to be about The Earth. Your project should be about whatever you are excited about!

How to make a great science fair project

  • Find any topic that interests you
  • Ask a question
  • Make a prediction
  • Do an experiment to answer your question
  • Write down the results
  • Make a poster to share your project
  • Review our 2021 Science Fair Information Packet

Remember: plan a safe experiment, free from extreme hazards, explosives, and infectious agents.
