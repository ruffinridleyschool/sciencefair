Calling all scientists!

NOTE: Fair Date Change
The theme of this year's fair is "The Earth", and the in-person Fair is Saturday, May 22nd (rain date the 23rd) at 8AM at the FRR School soccer field. The entire school, pre-K through 8th grade, even those who may have particpated in the virtual fair, are welcome to participate.

Fair Date/Time: May 22, 2021 at 8:00 AM

Due to uncertainties surrounding the pandemic, the date, format, and location (virtual/indoors/outdoors) of the main fair in May is subject to change. The PTO will announce any changes in fair date and location in the coming weeks. Though an in-person fair has been planned for the date and time above, if circumstances change, that fair could be held virtually.

Please note: your project does not have to be about The Earth. Your project should be about whatever you are excited about!

How to make a great science fair project

  • Find any topic that interests you
  • Ask a question
  • Make a prediction
  • Do an experiment to answer your question
  • Write down the results
  • Make a poster to share your project
  • Review our 2021 Science Fair Information Packet

Remember: plan a safe experiment, free from extreme hazards, explosives, and infectious agents.
